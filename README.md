# Restaurant Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This project analyzes restaurant sales data using Tableau Public to identify 
revenue trends, top products, cities, and payment methods.

## Business Objective
Support data-driven decisions to improve sales performance and profitability.

## Dataset
The dataset contains sales transactions including dates, cities, products, price, Quantity 
payment methods, and revenue.

## Tools Used
- Tableau Public
- Data Visualization
- KPI Design

## KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Top profit Product 
- Top Revenue City

## Key Insights
- Revenue shows strong peaks indicating seasonality.
- Lisbon is the highest revenue-generating city.
- Beverages lead in product sales.
- Online orders and credit cards are the most common payment methods.
